Teacher Workflow

  1. Create your teaching subjects.
  2. Add your students to the system.
  3. Assign RFID cards to each student.
  4. Enroll students to their subject.
  5. Start an attendance session.
  6. Students scan their RFID cards.
  7. The system records attendance automatically.

System Features

  • RFID-based student identification
  • Live classroom attendance monitoring
  • Secure teacher login (Email or RFID)
  • Automatic attendance records
  • Subject and student management
  • Designed for scalability and reliability
